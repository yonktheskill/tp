- **PingBook** is a desktop contact management application targeted at university students juggling multiple courses, projects, and deadlines.
    - It is optimised for users who prefer a **CLI-driven workflow**.
- **Target user:** Computer Science students at all levels, from those new to command-line tools to experienced terminal users.
- **Value proposition:** PingBook helps overwhelmed students manage their contacts by providing fast, typed commands to add, search, and organise contacts — reducing mental load and improving follow-through.

**Key features:**
- **Creating/Editing contacts** — `add`, `edit`, `remark`, `delete`
- **Finding contacts** — `list`, `find` (across names, phone numbers, emails, addresses, remark, and tags), `filter` (by tag), `sort` (starred first, then alphabetical)
- **Organising contacts** — `star`/`unstar` to pin important contacts, `archive`/`unarchive`/`listarchived` to hide contacts you rarely need
- **Command aliases** — create personal shortcuts (e.g. `a` for `add`) to speed up your workflow
